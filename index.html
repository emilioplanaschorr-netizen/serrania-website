Serranía Investments is a Colombian alternative investment firm. We specialize in structuring deals, sourcing projects to invest in, raising capital, and advising third parties on investment decisions.

We look where others won't. Our deep roots in Colombian soil — from the highland farms of Cundinamarca to the vast llanos — give us a perspective that no spreadsheet can replicate. We invest with the care of artisans and the conviction of those who know the land.

We believe in the extraordinary potential of Colombia.
