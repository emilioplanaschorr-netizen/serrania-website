Serranía Investments is a Colombian alternative investment firm. We specialize in structuring deals, sourcing real asset projects, raising capital, and providing strategic advisory on investment projects.

We look where others won't. Our deep roots in Colombian soil — from the highland farms of Cundinamarca to the vast llanos — give us a perspective that no spreadsheet can replicate. We invest with the care of artisans and the conviction of those who know the land.

We believe in the extraordinary potential of Colombia.

What We Do

FOCUS AREAS

DEAL STRUCTURING

We design and structure investment vehicles tailored to each project's unique characteristics, ensuring alignment between investors, operators, and long-term value creation.

PROJECT SOURCING

With boots on the ground across Colombia, we identify high-potential real asset projects — agriculture, natural resources, and infrastructure — where structural advantages create outsized returns.

CAPITAL RAISING

We work alongside project sponsors to connect compelling Colombian opportunities with qualified domestic and international investors who share a long-term vision for the country.

STRATEGIC ADVISORY

Drawing on deep consulting expertise, we advise third parties on the strategic and operational aspects of investment projects, including due diligence, deal evaluation, and market analysis in Colombia.
